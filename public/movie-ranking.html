Dylan's Favourite TV Shows of All Time

Non-Animated

Game of Thrones

I don't care how many people complain about the last season or the ending... this is the greatest show of all time. Front to back. Start to finish. Simply amazing.

Breaking Bad

Starts out a little slow in the first season, but then really picks up steam. Great show. Highly Recommend.

Sons of Anarchy

Awesome show. Corny at times, but great television.
